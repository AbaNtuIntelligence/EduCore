## 📋 Overview

EDUCORE is a **premium B2B e-commerce platform** for stationery, office furniture, PPE, and hygiene supplies. Built for schools, clinics, government institutions, and businesses across South Africa.

### 🎯 Key Objectives

- **Modern Catalogue**: Browse products with filtering and search
- **Quote Management**: Add products to a quote basket and request pricing
- **Admin Dashboard**: Full CRUD operations for product management
- **Professional Branding**: Premium design with corporate identity

---

## ✨ Features

### 🏠 **Homepage**
- Full-width hero slider with rotating images
- Product category grid with hover effects
- Product showcase carousel with auto-play
- Trust badges and client logos
- Call-to-action sections

### 📦 **Catalogue**
- Product grid with image, price, and stock status
- Category filtering (Stationery, Furniture, PPE, Hygiene)
- Search functionality
- Grid/List view toggle
- "Add to Quote" functionality
- Downloadable PDF catalogue

### 🛒 **Quote System**
- Persistent quote basket with Zustand
- Quantity management
- Professional quote request form
- Email notifications to team
- Client confirmation emails

### 👨‍💼 **Admin Dashboard**
- Secure login (JWT authentication)
- Product CRUD operations
- Image URL management
- Category management
- Featured product toggle

### 📄 **Content Pages**
- **About Us**: Company overview with visuals
- **Tender Support**: Capabilities and compliance
- **Contact**: Form with Google Maps integration
- **PDF Catalogue**: Print-ready product catalogue
